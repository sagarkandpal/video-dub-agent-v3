Under the hood

What happens after you hit start

DIARIZE

Speakers, separated

Pyannote splits the audio by who's talking and estimates each speaker's pitch to keep genders matched in the dub.

TRANSCRIBE + TRANSLATE

Meaning, carried over

Sarvam or Whisper transcribes each segment to English, then Groq translates it into natural, spoken-style target language.

DUB + SYNC

Voices, re-recorded

Each speaker gets a consistent, gender-matched voice, time-stretched to fit the original pacing and muxed back onto picture.
